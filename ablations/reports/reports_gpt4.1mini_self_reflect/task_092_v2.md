# Comprehensive Report on Admission Requirements for Master’s in Economics at Université Paris-Saclay and France Excellence Charpak Master’s Scholarship (2026-2027 Academic Year)

## 1. Admission Requirements and Eligibility Criteria for the Master’s in Economics at Université Paris-Saclay (2026-2027)

### 1.1 Program Overview

The Master’s in Economics at Université Paris-Saclay is a two-year graduate program consisting of Master 1 (M1) and Master 2 (M2) years. The program is fully taught in English and is designed to provide rigorous training in economic theory, applied econometrics, public and behavioral economics, macroeconomics, and quantitative methods such as programming and data analysis.

The program prepares students for academic research (PhD), public and private sector roles including consulting, data science, and policy analysis.

- **Duration:** 2 years (M1 + M2)  
- **Language of Instruction:** English (French not mandatory)  
- **Specializations:** Includes tracks in Trade and Development, Public and Behavioral Economics, Macroeconomics, and others in the second year.  
- **Career Paths:** Academia, international organizations, public policy, consulting, and data-driven roles  
- **Delivery:** Lectures, seminars, project work, and a master’s thesis in M2  
(Source: [Université Paris-Saclay M1 Economics](http://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/en/education/masters-degree/economics/m1-economics) [1]; [Université Paris-Saclay M2 Economics](http://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/en/education/masters-degree/economics/m2-economics) [2])

### 1.2 Academic Qualifications and Eligibility

Applicants must meet the following academic criteria:

- Hold a Bachelor’s degree (BA or BSc) in Economics, Mathematics, Statistics, or closely related quantitative disciplines.  
- Academic background must include solid training in microeconomics, macroeconomics, econometrics, calculus, statistics, and quantitative methods.  
- Candidates with degrees outside the exact fields may be considered upon strong analytical and quantitative coursework evidence.  
- Equivalent foreign degrees are accepted after validation through the application process.  
(Source: [Université Paris-Saclay International Students Admission](http://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/en/admission/etudiants-internationaux/international-students-applying-masters-degree) [3])

### 1.3 Language Proficiency Requirements

- The program is fully in English; all applicants from non-native English-speaking backgrounds must provide proof of English proficiency.  
- Accepted tests include TOEFL, IELTS, TOEIC with typical minimum requirements around B2 level of CEFR. For example:  
  - TOEIC Listening & Reading: minimum 850  
  - TOEIC Speaking: minimum 160  
  - TOEIC Writing: minimum 150  
- No French proficiency is required for admission although it is encouraged for campus life and integration.  
(Source: [Université Paris-Saclay Admissions](http://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/en/admission/masters-applications-and-enrolment) [4]; [AI Master Admissions Details](https://ai-master.lisn.upsaclay.fr/admissions/details-about-the-application/) [5])

### 1.4 Application Procedures

- Applications for the 2026-2027 intake for M1 open January 15 and close March 16, 2026.  
- Applications are submitted exclusively through the **INCEPTION platform** ([https://inception.universite-paris-saclay.fr/](https://inception.universite-paris-saclay.fr/)) designed for international Master’s degree applicants.  
- Steps include:  
  1. Create an account and complete the online application.  
  2. Upload required documents (see below).  
  3. Await admission decision, typically communicated within 6-8 weeks of application deadline.  
- No separate entrance exams or interviews for the Economics program are required. Admission is based on academic records, motivation, and supporting documents.  
(Source: [Université Paris-Saclay Masters Application](http://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/en/admission/masters-applications-and-enrolment) [4]; [INCEPTION Portal](https://inception.universite-paris-saclay.fr/fr/) [6])

### 1.5 Application Required Documents

Applicants must upload the following:  

- Bachelor's degree diploma and all academic transcripts (official, certified copies with English or French translation if needed).  
- Curriculum Vitae (CV) showcasing academic and extracurricular achievements.  
- Motivation letter explaining academic interests, career goals, and reasons for choosing Université Paris-Saclay’s Economics Master’s program.  
- Two academic recommendation letters (especially if applicant has not studied in France recently).  
- Proof of English language proficiency (TOEFL, IELTS, or equivalent).  
- Copy of valid passport or national ID.  
(Source: [Université Paris-Saclay Application Instructions](http://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/en/admission/masters-applications-and-enrolment) [4]; [Campus France India Charpak Scholarship Document List](https://inde.campusfrance.org/charpak-master-s-scholarship) [7])

### 1.6 Application Deadlines Summary

- Master 1 (M1) Economics application period: **January 15 – March 16, 2026**  
- Scholarship application deadlines for related scholarships and university scholarships typically coincide or fall within March 2026.  
- Late applications are generally not accepted, hence timely submission is critical.  
(Source: [Université Paris-Saclay Admissions 2026](http://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/en/admission/masters-applications-and-enrolment) [4])

### 1.7 Enrollment and Registration Process upon Admission

Once admitted, the following steps are required:  

- Confirm admission via the INCEPTION platform or linked university portals.  
- Pay or obtain exemption for the mandatory **Student and Campus Life Contribution (CVEC)** (~€95 in 2026).  
- Complete administrative registration via IA-PRIMO platform for first-time enrollees or APOWEB for returning students.  
- Upload required final documents through the PJWEB platform.  
- Register academically for courses and examinations in the academic management system.  
- International students are supported by the university’s International Welcome Desk for visa guidance, accommodation assistance, and integration programs.  
(Source: [Université Paris-Saclay Enrollment Information](http://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/en/information-about-registration) [8])

## 2. France Excellence Charpak Master’s Scholarship for Indian Students (2026-2027 Intake)

### 2.1 Overview

The France Excellence Charpak Master’s Scholarship is a prestigious scholarship offered by the French Embassy in India and facilitated by Campus France India. It supports Indian nationals seeking a full-time Master’s degree program in France, including Université Paris-Saclay’s Master’s in Economics.

- Scholarship duration: 1 or 2 years depending on the program length.  
- Covers living expenses, visa facilitation, and health coverage.  
(Source: [Campus France India Charpak Scholarship](https://inde.campusfrance.org/charpak-master-s-scholarship) [7])

### 2.2 Eligibility Criteria for Indian Applicants

- Must hold Indian citizenship or Overseas Citizen of India (OCI) status.  
- Must be aged 30 years or younger at the time of application.  
- Must have completed or be enrolled in a recognized Indian higher education institution (priority is given to students with Indian academic backgrounds).  
- Must have applied or possess admission confirmation to a full-time Master’s degree program conducted entirely in France (Master’s programs taught in English or French are both eligible).  
- French language proficiency is advantageous but not mandatory for the scholarship.  
- Applicants should not hold any other major scholarship exceeding €600/month.  
(Source: [France Excellence Charpak Scholarship Eligibility](https://inde.campusfrance.org/charpak-master-s-scholarship) [7]; [Truescho Charpak Details](https://truescho.com/en/opportunities/france-excellence-charpak-master-scholarship) [9])

### 2.3 Application Requirements and Documents

Applicants must submit their application through the official Campus France India platform. Required documents include:  

- Copy of valid passport  
- Admission proof or application acknowledgment from the French university including Université Paris-Saclay  
- Academic transcripts and diplomas (Bachelor’s and any relevant studies)  
- Curriculum Vitae (CV)  
- Statement of Purpose (SOP) detailing academic motivations and career ambitions; originality is strictly enforced, AI-generated text is prohibited.  
- Two academic recommendation letters  
- English and/or French language certificates if available (not mandatory)  
- Any additional documents requested during the application process  
(Source: [Campus France India Charpak Scholarship Application Info](https://inde.campusfrance.org/charpak-master-s-scholarship) [7])

### 2.4 Application Deadlines and Selection Process

- **Application deadline:** March 30, 2026 (23:59 IST)  
- **Interview period:** Mandatory online interviews conducted from April 13 – April 30, 2026  
- After the interview, final selection is based on academic merit, clarity of motivation, consistency between academic and professional plans, and interview performance.  
- Results are usually announced by the end of May or early June 2026.  
- The process is highly competitive and requires thorough preparation.  
(Source: [Campus France India Charpak Timeline](https://inde.campusfrance.org/charpak-master-s-scholarship) [7]; [Leap Scholar Charpak 2026](https://leapscholar.com/blog/france-excellence-charpak-master-scholarship/) [10])

### 2.5 Scholarship Benefits

- Monthly stipend of approximately **€860** to cover living expenses.  
- Exemption from visa fee for Indian applicants.  
- Waiver of Campus France application fees.  
- Coverage of French student social security (health insurance).  
- Support for finding affordable housing through CROUS and access to student cultural services.  
- Tuition fees are generally exempted or significantly reduced at French public universities.  
- Scholarship is renewable for the second year subject to satisfactory academic progress.  
- Travel costs and personal expenses are not covered.  
(Source: [Buddy4Study Charpak Scholarship Benefits](https://www.buddy4study.com/scholarship/france-excellence-charpak-master-s-program-2025) [11]; [Campus France India Benefits](https://inde.campusfrance.org/charpak-master-s-scholarship) [7])

## 3. Other Scholarships for Master’s in Economics Students at Université Paris-Saclay (2026-2027)

### 3.1 Université Paris-Saclay International Master’s Scholarships

Université Paris-Saclay offers a merit-based International Master’s Scholarship Program available to outstanding international students admitted to master’s degree programs, including Economics.

- Eligibility:  
  - International students (including Indian nationals) under 30 years old.  
  - Admitted to an M1 or M2 Master’s program at Université Paris-Saclay or its affiliated institutions.  
  - Not previously enrolled full-time in a French public university (with some possible exceptions).  
  - Should not hold other major scholarships providing monthly allowances above €600.  
- Application:  
  - Admission to the university before March 25, 2026.  
  - Nomination by program coordinators after admission.  
  - Scholarship application submitted by invited candidates by March 31, 2026.  
  - Requires a motivation letter and two recommendation letters focused on academic excellence and research potential.  
- Benefits:  
  - Annual scholarship of €10,000 paid over 10 months (September to June).  
  - Up to €900 towards travel and visa expenses.  
  - Duration: 1 year (if entering M2) or 2 years (if entering M1), renewable subject to academic progress.  
  - University support services for housing, visa, and welcoming international students.  
- Focuses especially on students motivated to pursue research or PhD trajectories.  
(Source: [Université Paris-Saclay International Scholarship Program](https://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/en/education/masters-degree/international-masters-scholarship) [12]; [Scholars4Dev Paris-Saclay Scholarship](https://www.scholars4dev.com/15259/universite-paris-saclay-international-masters-scholarships-2/) [13])

### 3.2 Other Scholarships and Financial Aid Options

- **Eiffel Excellence Scholarship:** Mainly for engineering, science, and management fields, highly competitive, nominal monthly allowance (~€1,200), does not cover tuition fully, may be accessible for some economics-related fields but not primary target.  
- **Erasmus+ Mobility Grants:** For joint or exchange programs; do not cover full master’s tuition or living costs but can complement other funding.  
- **Indian Government and Private Scholarships:** Such as National Overseas Scholarship and Inlaks Shivdasani Foundation may support Indian students studying abroad, but not specifically linked to Paris-Saclay or Economics programs.  
- **University Mobility and Merit Scholarships:** Occasionally available for specific research projects, internships, or as part of departmental funding, but limited and on case-by-case basis.  
(Source: [Eiffel Scholarship Details](https://www.campusfrance.org/en/eiffel-scholarship-program-of-excellence) [14]; [Université Paris-Saclay Scholarships Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/opportunitiescircleofficial/posts/university-of-paris-saclay-scholarship-in-france-2026-fully-fundedapply-here-htt/1516401613858356/) [15])

## 4. Summary and Recommendations for Prospective Indian Students

- Prospective Indian students must have a strong quantitative and economics background, English proficiency, and submit a detailed application by March 16, 2026, for M1 Economics via the INCEPTION platform.  
- Required documents include academic transcripts, CV, motivation letter, two academic references, English proficiency proofs, and passport copies.  
- For financial support, the France Excellence Charpak Scholarship is the primary dedicated funding option for Indian students, with applications closing March 30, 2026, followed by mandatory interviews in April.  
- The Université Paris-Saclay International Master’s Scholarship is another key program offering €10,000/year, with applications by invitation only after university admission.  
- Multiple scholarships can be pursued independently, as eligibility and benefits vary.  
- Early preparation of original motivation letters, securing strong academic references, and demonstrating a clear research or career vision substantially improve admission and scholarship chances.  
- Admitted students must complete enrollment steps including CVEC payment and administrative registration through designated platforms before starting their studies.  
- Additional language preparation in English is strongly advised; French proficiency, while not required, is useful for campus living and integration.

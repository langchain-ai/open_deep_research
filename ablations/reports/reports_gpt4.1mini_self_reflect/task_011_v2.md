## 2. Overview of Key Entities

### 2.1 Mesa Group Consulting and Evert Calderon

Mesa Group Consulting is a Bakersfield, California-based business consultancy founded in 2023 that focuses extensively on credit repair, credit monitoring, business funding, and personalized financial advisory services. Evert Calderon, the company owner and Visionary Operations Officer, holds certifications in credit repair and has expertise in blending fintech solutions with digital marketing to serve diverse communities, including underserved and multilingual (English, Spanish, Punjabi) demographics.

Their business model revolves around enhancing clients’ financial health through credit rebuilding, loan facilitation, and business credit development, monetizing via affiliate commissions and possibly white-label programs.

### 2.2 SuperMoney: Fintech Marketplace and Affiliate Network

- **Company Profile:** Founded in 2013, headquartered in Santa Ana, California, SuperMoney has evolved from a personal finance blog into an expansive fintech marketplace offering consumer and small business loan comparisons and applications.

- **Product Range:** Offers credit cards, personal loans, auto refinancing, student loan refinancing, debt consolidation loans, debt relief services, business financing, trust and will plans, life insurance, credit monitoring, and tax services. The platform facilitates easy, soft-credit-check prequalifications and comparison-based decision-making.

- **Affiliate Program:** Enables partners to earn commissions via qualified leads and funded loan conversions. Commissions range from approximately $15 per credit report to up to $750 per funded loan, with potential revenue sharing reaching 80% for business financing sales. Tools include widgets, co-branded offer engines, and embeddable applications.

- **Awards & Recognition:** Consistently honored on Inc. 5000, Deloitte Technology Fast 500, and recipient of “Best Financial Product Comparison Service” (2023, 2024) [1][2][3].

### 2.3 Novae: Fintech Plus Direct Sales and White Label Platform

- **Company Profile:** Established in 2014 and based in Conyers, Georgia, Novae operates as a fintech company with a multi-channel business model blending affiliate marketing, AI-powered credit tools, and white-label fintech services.

- **Product Range:** Comprehensive consumer and business financial products including credit cards, personal loans, auto loan refinancing, student loan refinancing, debt consolidation, debt relief (personal and business), life insurance, trust and will estate planning, tax preparation with refund advances, savings, no-interest cash advances, and business credit/funding solutions up to $10 million.

- **Affiliate Program:** Operates a tiered system (Affiliate BASIC and PRO), offering high-potential commissions on personal sales, customer acquisition bonuses, residual income, and multi-level team commissions. The program includes detailed compliance and legal requirements, monthly fees, and offers complete fintech white-label solutions for business partners.

- **Social Impact:** Novae directs a percentage of affiliate revenues to “Novae Cares,” supporting underserved communities. It holds repeated recognition on the Inc. 5000 list for fastest-growing private companies [4][5][6].

## 4. Business Models and Affiliate Program Structures

### 4.1 SuperMoney Affiliate Model

SuperMoney monetizes through:

- **CPA and Revenue Share Commissions:** Affiliates earn per funded loan or successful lead with commissions scaled by product type; credit cards and personal loans provide $15–$200+ per lead, while business loans can yield revenue shares up to 80%.

- **Affiliate Tools:** Partners receive access to embed product widgets, co-branded offer engines, and API-driven prequalification platforms to integrate seamlessly into websites.

- **Free Enrollment and Network Expansion:** The program supports hundreds of affiliate partners, emphasizing high conversion rates via user-friendly digital applications [1][2].

### 4.2 Novae Affiliate and White Label Model

Novae’s affiliate program operates on a **subscription and performance-driven model** with:

- **Two Tiers:**

  - *Affiliate BASIC:* $199 enrollment + $49/month, with commissions on personal sales only.

  - *Affiliate PRO:* $349 enrollment + $99/month, includes team commissions, residual income, credit monitoring tools, and business development training.

- **Multiple Commission Streams:**

  - Direct sales commissions (up to $3,000 per referral in some cases).

  - Customer Acquisition Bonuses (CABs) awarded for each qualifying affiliate or client within 60 days.

  - Multi-level overrides up to 10 generations deep.

- **White Label Program:** Businesses may license Novae fintech platforms with branding rights, software, and customer support—allowing revenue via markups and service fees.

- **Social Responsibility:** The model supports social causes through revenue-sharing commitments (Novae Cares) [4][5][6].

### 4.3 Mesa Group Consulting’s Monetization Approach

Mesa Group Consulting complements its credit repair and business consulting expertise by partnering with these affiliate programs:

- **Referral Commissions:** Earns revenue for each client referred who completes a qualifying action (application, funded loan, service signup).

- **Affiliate Integration:** Although detailed technical implementation is not publicly disclosed, Mesa Group likely uses affiliate links, embedded offer widgets, and co-branded marketing assets on dedicated product pages.

- **White Label Utilization:** Potential leveraging of Novae’s white label fintech platform enables Mesa Group to offer branded financial services in business credit and funding, expanding service capabilities and revenue streams.

- **Value-Added Service:** By aligning product promotion with expert consulting services, Mesa Group enhances conversion probabilities and client satisfaction, thereby increasing affiliate income indirectly.

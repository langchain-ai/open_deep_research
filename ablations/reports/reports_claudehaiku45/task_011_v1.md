# How Evert Calderon Leverages SuperMoney and Novae for Mesa Group Consulting: A Comprehensive Analysis

## Executive Summary

Evert Calderon operates Mesa Group Consulting as an affiliate marketing company that strategically partners with leading financial services platforms to provide credit repair, credit monitoring, business funding, and related financial services to underserved communities. The company has established documented affiliate relationships with SuperMoney and Novae Money, along with other partners like SmartCredit, to monetize financial product referrals across multiple categories. Mesa Group Consulting generates revenue through commission-based affiliate relationships rather than direct service provision, positioning itself as a consulting intermediary that connects clients with third-party service providers and earns compensation when those clients enroll in services through Mesa Group's referral links.

## Part 1: SuperMoney Business Model and Affiliate Structure

### SuperMoney Platform Overview

[SuperMoney.com](https://www.supermoney.com/) operates as a comprehensive financial services marketplace and comparison platform, functioning similarly to KAYAK for financial products.[1] Founded in 2013 and led by CEO Miron Lulic, SuperMoney has surpassed $1 billion in financing requests submitted through its platform.[1] The company generates revenue through a cost-per-click (CPC) or cost-per-acquisition (CPA) model, meaning it earns money when users click through to financial products or complete applications.[1]

SuperMoney's core value proposition is providing transparent, consumer-friendly comparisons across financial services while maintaining strict data security compliance (SOC 2 Type II certification).[1] The company emphasizes that it does not sell customer data to the highest bidder; instead, it works directly with lending partners to provide real-time pre-approved offers to consumers.[1]

### Financial Products Available Through SuperMoney

SuperMoney aggregates access to over 50 financial service verticals, enabling affiliates like Mesa Group Consulting to monetize across diverse product categories.[1] The specific products available include:

**Lending Products:**
- Personal loans with payouts up to $750 per funded loan through their affiliate program
- Auto loans and auto refinancing, with a marketplace featuring roughly 70 finance companies including subprime specialists (Credit Acceptance, Exeter Finance), captives (GM Financial, Ford Motor Credit, Toyota Financial Services), commercial banks (Bank of America, Wells Fargo), and market leaders (Ally Financial, Chase Auto Finance)
- Student loan refinancing with real-time quotes from multiple lenders including LendKey, CommonBond, and SoFi
- Debt consolidation loans available up to $100,000 for all credit types
- Credit cards with competing offers

**Non-Lending Financial Products:**
- Banking and checking accounts
- Savings accounts
- Insurance products (auto, home, life, travel)
- Investment products
- Mortgages
- Tax preparation services

### SuperMoney's Affiliate Program Structure

SuperMoney operates multiple affiliate distribution channels allowing companies like Mesa Group Consulting to monetize these financial products:

**Direct Monetization Solutions:** SuperMoney offers three primary monetization tools through its website:[1]

1. **Money Marketplace** - A turnkey, co-branded offer engine that delivers instant competing loan offers while maintaining the partner's branding front and center. The Money Marketplace includes embeddable widgets customizable to the partner's branding, allowing site visitors to pre-qualify for loans in real-time without leaving the page.

2. **Pre-qualification Platform** - Allows users to pre-qualify for personal loans in real-time without leaving a partner's website, streamlining the user experience.

3. **Super Links** - Enables websites and content creators to monetize by referring traffic to over 50 financial service verticals through simple tracking links that integrate into existing content. SuperMoney provides a turnkey, co-branded page listing related, alternate financial service offers that users can compare and select from.

**FlexOffers.com Distribution Channel:** SuperMoney also distributes affiliate programs through [FlexOffers.com](https://www.flexoffers.com/), an award-winning affiliate marketing network.[1] Multiple SuperMoney affiliate programs are hosted on FlexOffers, including:
- SuperMoney Personal Loans Affiliate Program
- SuperMoney Auto Loans Affiliate Program
- SuperMoney Student Loan Refinancing Affiliate Program
- SuperMoney Credit Cards Affiliate Program
- SuperMoney Banking Affiliate Program
- SuperMoney Investing Affiliate Program

### SuperMoney Commission Rates and Payment Terms

SuperMoney advertises "industry leading commissions" across its monetization solutions, though specific rates vary by product type and partnership arrangement.[1]

**Published Commission Rates:**
- **Personal Loans:** Up to $750 per funded loan through FlexOffers, with the advantage that affiliates earn commissions per qualified lead rather than waiting for full loan funding
- **Investing:** Up to $25 cost-per-acquisition (CPA)
- **Other Product Categories:** Specific commission rates for credit cards, auto loans, student loan refinancing, and debt consolidation are not uniformly published and are subject to individual partnership agreements

The company emphasizes that personal loan commissions represent some of the highest earnings per click (EPC) in the affiliate marketing space due to SuperMoney's direct integration with many lenders, resulting in higher conversion rates than single-lender platforms.

### SuperMoney Affiliate Program Access and Requirements

SuperMoney provides multiple pathways for partner onboarding:

**Direct Partnership:** Prospective partners interested in custom or unique partnership arrangements beyond standard programs can contact SuperMoney at partners@supermoney.com with company details and partnership proposals. If the proposal aligns with SuperMoney's interests, their partnership team responds accordingly.[1]

**FlexOffers Channel:** Registration for affiliates and publishers on FlexOffers.com is free and accessible online, providing a streamlined onboarding process.[1]

**Key Differentiators:** SuperMoney's affiliate program stands out through several features that benefit partners:
- Wide network of hundreds of the US's top financial brands
- Access to over 50 financial service verticals for diverse monetization opportunities
- Direct lender integration rather than lead auction systems, ensuring consumers don't receive excessive robocalls from lead buyers
- No data selling practices, maintaining consumer trust and repeat business
- Real-time offer generation
- Co-branded pages maintaining partner branding
- Extensive financial content and articles provided to partners for content monetization and expansion into new finance categories
- Multiple industry recognitions including "Best Financial Product Comparison Service" Award, four consecutive appearances on Inc. 5000 List, and inclusion on Deloitte's 2022 Technology Fast 500 List

## Part 2: Novae Money Business Model and Affiliate Structure

### Novae Company Overview and Mission

[Novae](https://www.novaemoney.com) is a fintech company founded in 2014 and headquartered in Conyers, Georgia, that provides greater access to credit, capital, and entrepreneurship for consumers and small businesses nationwide, with particular focus on empowering underserved communities and driving financial inclusion.[2] The company serves clients across all 50 states, including Puerto Rico.[2]

Novae has been recognized as one of America's fastest-growing companies, appearing on the Inc. 5000 list of America's Fastest-Growing Private Companies for five consecutive years (2021-2025).[2] The company has received media coverage from Forbes, Yahoo Finance, and Black Enterprise, with endorsements from notable figures including Daymond John.[2]

Novae is a proud member of the Direct Selling Association and a Signatory of the DSA Code of Ethics, providing regulatory oversight and ethical business practice standards.[2] The company's mission centers on three pillars: INSPIRING people to get more out of life, EDUCATING them on ways to achieve it, and providing an OPPORTUNITY to help them make it happen.[2] A percentage of all revenue from the Affiliate Program sales go toward the Novae Cares initiative, which supports education, displaced families, elderly communities, and basic needs like food and clothing.[2]

### Novae Affiliate Program Structure and Enrollment Tiers

Novae operates a multi-tiered affiliate program with three enrollment options, each providing different levels of services, benefits, and compensation opportunities:

**Affiliate Basic Tier:** $199 upfront fee plus $49 monthly
- Focuses on direct customer sales and personal commissions only
- Includes marketing tools and training materials
- Does not include team-building commissions or rank advancement benefits
- Designed for individuals primarily generating revenue through personal sales

**Affiliate Pro Tier:** $349 upfront fee plus $99 monthly
- Includes myNovaeCredit Monitoring service
- Includes myNovaeDisputes Manager for credit report dispute assistance
- Provides comprehensive marketing tools and training
- Enables team override commissions for building and supporting a successful downline
- Allows rank advancement awards including residual bonuses, car payments, and private stock options
- Exclusive override commissions reward members for building and supporting successful teams

**Premium Tier:** $499 upfront fee with $50 monthly fee
- Covers website maintenance, advanced business tools, and affiliate discounts
- Highest level of support and features

Novae also offers a White Label Partner Program for entrepreneurs and existing businesses seeking to license Novae's software, processes, and brand infrastructure. This program requires a system infrastructure setup fee ranging from $10,000 to $25,000 depending on selected services and can generate up to 400% higher commissions than co-brand partners.[2]

### Novae Commission and Compensation Structure

Novae's compensation model is significantly more complex than traditional affiliate programs, incorporating multiple revenue streams:

**Personal Commissions:** Every successful product or service sale generates a Personal Commission specific to that offering.[2] Commissions vary by product and service sold, with examples including:
- Up to $100 per referral on Novae Money credit education service
- Up to $500 per customer enrolled depending on the specific service
- Commissions on life insurance, debt relief, business credit, and other services

**Customer Acquisition Bonuses (CABs):** When new affiliates make qualifying sales within 30 days of enrollment, they receive CAB bonuses. CABs are paid when new affiliates complete two Qualifying CAB Actions (QCAs) within 60 days. CAB commissions scale by affiliate rank and enrollment level, with commissions reaching up to $600+ for qualified affiliates.[2]

**Override Commissions:** Available exclusively to Affiliate Pro members, override commissions provide earnings based on sales made by affiliates in their downline, extending down through ten generations.[2] This structure enables team-building revenue. Up to $200 in overrides from referred affiliates are possible depending on performance and rank.[2]

**Monthly Residual Income:** Affiliates earn residual income based on sales volume, with rank advancement determined by group sales, qualified legs, and personal customer points.[2] Monthly residual commissions can reach up to $110,000 for top levels, though achievement requires specific sales volumes and qualified business legs.[2] The "five & free" benefit allows affiliates to waive their monthly fee by enrolling or selling to at least five customers or affiliates monthly.[2]

**Novae University Training Commissions:** Novae hosts live events called Novae University providing training and personal growth seminars, and affiliates earn commissions from registrations within their organization down five levels.[2]

**Financed Volume Commissions:** Affiliates earn commission based on financed clients on a monthly basis, with 20% of financed volume commission paid out.[2]

### Novae Payment Terms and Affiliate Requirements

**Payment Schedule:**
- Commissions are paid weekly for sales activities
- Monthly residuals are paid on the 15th of the following month
- Weekly commissions require a minimum threshold of $25 or $20 to be processed
- Affiliates must submit a W-9 and bank details to receive commission payments via direct deposit
- Commissions are held until W-9 forms are verified

**Affiliate Enrollment Requirements:**
- Must be at least 18 years of age
- Must reside in authorized countries
- Must provide valid tax identification
- Must submit a completed Affiliate Agreement
- Must personally sell Novae products or services
- No mandatory product purchases required to become an affiliate

**Agreement Terms:**
- Initial agreement lasts one year with automatic renewal unless notice is given
- If Novae does not notify applicants of acceptance within thirty (30) days, the application is deemed rejected
- Customer and affiliate placement within the network can be managed within the first 60 days to optimize commission structure
- Affiliates are independent contractors responsible for their own taxes and business decisions

### Novae Affiliate Support and Training

Novae provides comprehensive support to affiliates through:

**Novae University:** Live training and personal growth seminars for affiliate business development, with affiliates earning commissions on registrations within their organization

**Marketing Materials and Tools:** Extensive marketing rights, access to company support and events, replicated websites provided by Novae, and access to all business training and financial wellness program materials

**Members Portal and Resources:** Ongoing training and support through a members' portal, webinars, and social media

**Compliance and Marketing Standards:** All sales aids, promotional materials, advertisements, websites, and other literature must be submitted to Novae for written approval prior to use. Affiliates must avoid misleading, deceptive, or unauthorized claims about incomes, the compensation plan, or product results (such as guaranteeing credit repair). Income claims or representations on an affiliate's website are prohibited unless expressly authorized in writing by the Company with a link to the Novae Income Disclosure Statement immediately adjacent to any such claim.

### Income Disclosure and Earnings Data

According to Novae's published income disclosure, the average participant in Novae's affiliate business earned $1,408 in 2023.[2] The company notes that earnings naturally vary, with some earning less and others earning more based on dedication, effort, leadership, and fund allocation. Novae emphasizes that earning supplemental income results only from hard work, dedication, and leadership.

## Part 4: Comparative Analysis - SuperMoney vs. Novae as Affiliate Partners

### Business Model Differences

**SuperMoney** operates as a financial services marketplace and comparison platform with a B2B2C model. SuperMoney builds relationships with financial services providers (lenders, insurance companies, etc.) and then provides affiliate partners access to refer to those providers. Revenue flows from the financial services providers to SuperMoney when referrals result in applications or funded loans, and then SuperMoney shares a portion with affiliate partners like Mesa Group.

**Novae** operates as a fintech affiliate network and direct service provider with a direct affiliate model. Novae offers its own financial services (credit monitoring, debt relief assistance, business credit programs) AND provides an affiliate program allowing partners to earn commissions on those services. Novae also functions as an MLM-style network with multi-level commission structures.

### Product Portfolio and Category Depth

**SuperMoney** excels at breadth—offering access to 50+ financial service verticals with emphasis on comparison shopping and transparent pricing. Its strength is in lending products (personal loans, auto loans, student loans, debt consolidation) and related financial services where multiple lenders compete, driving transparent pricing and high conversion rates.

**Novae** offers a more curated but deeper suite focused on credit improvement, debt relief, and business services with emphasis on affiliate selling and team building. Novae's strength is in providing both the service delivery AND the sales organization, creating a vertically integrated affiliate marketing platform.

### Commission Models

**SuperMoney's** commission model is product-specific and transaction-based. Affiliates earn per funded loan, per application, per click, or per action depending on the product category. Rates appear to emphasize the highest commissions on personal loans ($750 per funded loan) where conversion friction is highest.

**Novae's** commission model is multi-faceted and volume-based, including personal commissions per sale, customer acquisition bonuses for new affiliates reaching sales targets, override commissions from downline sales, and monthly residual income based on group volume and rank. This model incentivizes team building and long-term volume over transaction-by-transaction sales.

### Affiliate Support and Training

**SuperMoney** provides co-branded pages, tracking links, embeddable widgets, and content support. The focus is on making it easy for partners to integrate financial products into existing content and funnels without extensive training or organizational support needed.

**Novae** provides Novae University live training events, members' portal support, webinars, social media engagement, and ongoing coaching. The model anticipates affiliates are building business operations and organizations rather than simply embedding product links.

### Regulatory and Compliance Posture

**SuperMoney** emphasizes consumer protection through SOC 2 Type II compliance, direct lender integration avoiding lead auction marketplaces, transparent pricing, and no data selling. The company operates as a licensed financial services platform subject to applicable regulations.

**Novae** emphasizes ethical business practices through Direct Selling Association membership and DSA Code of Ethics signatory status. The company provides income disclosure statements and documentation of affiliate requirements, policies, and procedures. However, the multi-level commission structure and team-building emphasis place Novae closer to traditional network marketing operating models.

### Scale and Market Presence

**SuperMoney** is a larger, more established platform (founded 2013, $1 billion+ in financed requests, Inc. 5000 recognition, Deloitte Technology Fast 500 list inclusion, multiple industry awards). It operates with significant venture capital backing and national brand recognition.

**Novae** is a growing fintech company (founded 2014, Inc. 5000 list for five consecutive years, media features) that operates with a more direct-to-consumer affiliate-driven model emphasizing grassroots team building rather than centralized brand awareness.

## Part 5: Strategic Integration into Mesa Group Consulting's Overall Business Strategy

### Market Positioning

Mesa Group Consulting's partnership strategy directly supports its core mission of serving underserved communities with language barriers and limited financial literacy. By partnering with SuperMoney and Novae—platforms with strong reputations and national reach—Mesa Group gains credibility and access to services that would be expensive and legally complex to provide directly.

The choice of both SuperMoney (B2B2C comparison model) and Novae (direct affiliate network model) is strategically complementary. SuperMoney provides access to transparent, competitive lending products ideal for clients seeking to understand options and compare terms. Novae provides deeper consultation and team-building opportunities for Mesa Group to potentially recruit additional affiliate partners, creating residual income streams aligned with Novae's compensation model.

### Revenue Diversification

By integrating SuperMoney across five lending categories (credit cards, personal loans, auto loans, student loans, debt consolidation) and Novae across five service categories (debt relief, estate planning, life insurance, business debt relief, business credit), Mesa Group has diversified its commission revenue sources across ten distinct product lines. This diversification reduces dependency on any single partner or product category and positions Mesa Group to serve varied client needs with multiple solutions.

### Compliance and Risk Mitigation

Mesa Group's affiliate model avoids the substantial regulatory, legal, and operational burden of directly providing credit repair (CROA compliance), lending (banking regulations), insurance (insurance regulations), or debt relief (FTC compliance) services. By routing clients through established, compliant platforms, Mesa Group mitigates legal and reputational risks while maintaining service delivery to clients.

### Community Empowerment Alignment

Both SuperMoney and Novae emphasize financial inclusion and serving underserved communities. SuperMoney highlights access for all credit types and transparency in lending, while Novae explicitly focuses on underserved communities and allocates affiliate revenue to community support (Novae Cares initiative). This values alignment strengthens Mesa Group's positioning as a mission-driven company rather than a purely transactional sales operation.

### Scalability Model

The affiliate model enables Mesa Group to scale revenue without proportional increases in headcount or infrastructure. As the company gains clients and referral volume through SuperMoney and Novae, commission revenue can grow substantially without needing to hire credit specialists, loan underwriters, or claims processors. The company's focus on free consultations and financial education creates high-volume, low-cost customer acquisition funnels into these affiliate programs.

Furthermore, Novae's affiliate program structure—with its multi-level commission opportunities and team-building incentives—provides a pathway for Mesa Group to potentially recruit its own downstream affiliates, creating additional revenue streams from other agents or organizations Mesa Group brings into the Novae network.

### Target Client Segmentation

Mesa Group's partnerships enable strategic client segmentation and routing:

- **Credit-Focused Clients:** Routed to SuperMoney's credit card and credit monitoring offerings, or to Novae's credit improvement and dispute management services
- **Funding-Seeking Clients:** Routed to SuperMoney's personal loans or auto loans, or to Novae's business funding and business credit services
- **Debt-Troubled Clients:** Routed to SuperMoney's debt consolidation or Novae's debt relief partnerships
- **Wealth-Building Clients:** Routed to Novae's life insurance, estate planning, and business credit services

This segmentation allows Mesa Group to present itself as a comprehensive financial consulting firm with appropriate solutions for different client situations, while revenue is generated through affiliate commissions on the product categories clients ultimately select.

## Part 6: Key Findings and Strategic Implications

### Primary Findings

1. **Explicit Partnership Disclosure:** Mesa Group Consulting publicly discloses its affiliate relationships with SuperMoney, Novae, and SmartCredit across multiple pages of its website, indicating transparency with clients about potential conflicts of interest and the referral-based business model.

2. **Complementary Affiliate Structures:** SuperMoney's transaction-based, comparison-shopping model complements Novae's team-building, multi-level affiliate model, allowing Mesa Group to serve different types of clients and capture different commission structures.

3. **Product Coverage Alignment:** The specific product categories identified on Mesa Group's project boards (credit cards, personal loans, auto loans, student loans, debt consolidation via SuperMoney; debt relief, estate planning, life insurance, business debt relief, business credit via Novae) cover the full spectrum of financial needs for underserved communities.

4. **Commission Model Opacity:** While SuperMoney's commission rates are somewhat documented ($750 per personal loan, $25 per investing referral), the specific rates Mesa Group receives—particularly for credit cards, auto loans, student loans, and debt consolidation—are not publicly disclosed. Similarly, Novae's commissions vary significantly based on customer segment (consumer vs. business), service type, and affiliate tier. Mesa Group likely negotiates custom commission rates based on its referral volume and strategic value to both platforms.

5. **Regulatory Advantage:** The affiliate model allows Mesa Group to offer a wide array of financial services while maintaining a lean legal and compliance footprint, avoiding the substantial regulatory burden of direct service provision.

### Strategic Implications for Mesa Group Consulting

**Strengths of Current Model:**
- Diversified revenue streams across multiple platforms and product categories
- Access to established, compliant service providers reducing risk
- Ability to serve comprehensive financial needs of underserved clients
- Scalable model enabling revenue growth without proportional cost increases
- Values alignment with both partner platforms around financial inclusion

**Potential Risks and Considerations:**
- Dependency on partner platforms for service delivery quality and client experience
- Commission rate fluctuations if partners restructure affiliate programs
- Client acquisition costs increasing as competitor affiliates saturate markets
- Limited control over product offerings, pricing, or terms offered to clients
- Reputational risk if partner platforms experience service failures or regulatory issues

**Optimization Opportunities:**
- Developing deeper Novae partnership with recruitment focus to build downstream affiliate teams for residual income
- Creating content and educational resources that organically drive traffic to affiliated products
- Segmenting client funnels by product category to optimize commission yields
- Building client relationships and follow-up systems to increase repeat engagement across product categories
- Expanding language offerings or geographic reach to maximize market opportunity in underserved communities

## Conclusion

Evert Calderon has built Mesa Group Consulting as a strategically focused affiliate marketing company that leverages partnerships with SuperMoney and Novae to serve underserved communities seeking credit repair, business funding, and comprehensive financial services. Rather than building proprietary services, Mesa Group functions as a trusted intermediary that routes clients to appropriate third-party providers while earning commissions when those clients enroll in services.

SuperMoney provides access to transparent, competitive lending products across five key categories (credit cards, personal loans, auto loans, student loans, debt consolidation) through a transaction-based affiliate model emphasizing application volume and conversion quality. Novae provides access to deeper financial services (debt relief, estate planning, life insurance, business debt relief, business credit) through a multi-level affiliate model emphasizing team building and residual income.

The strategic integration of both partners into Mesa Group's business model creates a diversified, scalable revenue base while positioning the company to serve comprehensive financial needs. The company's emphasis on transparency, community empowerment, multilingual service, and financial education distinguishes it from purely transactional affiliate operations, aligning its positioning with both partner platforms' values around financial inclusion.

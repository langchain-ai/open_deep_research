# Admission Requirements and France Excellence Charpak Scholarship for Master’s in Economics at Université Paris-Saclay (2025)

## 1. Admission Requirements and Eligibility for Master’s in Economics at Université Paris-Saclay

### 1.1 Program Overview

The Master’s in Economics at Université Paris-Saclay is a selective two-year graduate program divided into two parts: Master 1 (M1) and Master 2 (M2). It is taught entirely in English, designed to provide advanced training in economic theory, quantitative methods, and applied economics. The program prepares students for research careers, PhD programs, or high-level professional roles.

- **Duration:** 2 years (M1 and M2); M2 offers specialized tracks  
- **Language:** English  
- **Delivery:** Combination of lectures, projects, presentations, and written research papers  
- **Specializations (M2):** Trade and Development, Public and Behavioral Economics, Macroeconomics, and interdisciplinary options  
- **Exchange Opportunities:** Partnerships with European universities such as Humboldt University of Berlin and University of Bologna  
(Source: [Université Paris-Saclay Master’s in Economics](http://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/en/education/masters-degree/economics))

### 1.2 Academic and Prerequisite Qualifications

- **Degree Required:** A Bachelor’s degree (BA or BSc) in Economics, Mathematics, or closely related quantitative fields is mandatory.  
- **Academic Background:** Strong foundation in microeconomics, macroeconomics, econometrics, mathematics (including calculus and statistics), and quantitative methods is expected.  
- **Additional Expectations:** While no explicit prerequisite course list is available, applicants should possess competencies equivalent to advanced undergraduate economics and quantitative coursework.  
(Source: [CEPS Paris-Saclay Syllabus 2024-2025](https://www.ceps-paris-saclay.fr/fileadmin/user_upload/CEPS/Master_of_Economics_Syllabus_2024-2025.pdf))

### 1.3 Language Proficiency

- **English:** Since the program is fully taught in English, proof of English proficiency is required from non-native English speakers. Accepted qualifications include TOEFL and IELTS tests.  
- **Required Level:** Although exact score requirements are not formally published, applicants need proficiency sufficient for graduate-level study and academic research communication.  
- **French:** Not mandatory for the Economics program but can be an asset in daily life and integration.  
(Source: [Université Paris-Saclay Admissions](http://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/en/admission/etudiants-internationaux/international-students-applying-masters-degree))

### 1.4 Application Process

- **Platform:** Applications are submitted online exclusively through the INCEPTION portal managed by Université Paris-Saclay ([INCEPTION Portal](https://inception.universite-paris-saclay.fr/fr/)).  
- **Application Period:** For the 2025-2026 academic year, applications typically open in February and close between mid-May and late May, with some program-specific deadlines (e.g., M1 Economics applications often close in March).  
- **Documents Required:**  
  - Academic transcripts and diplomas from prior higher education  
  - Curriculum Vitae (CV)  
  - Motivation letter specifying reasons for choosing the program  
  - At least two letters of recommendation  
  - Proof of English proficiency (if applicable)  
  - Passport or ID copy for international students  
- **Additional Requirements for International Students:**  
  - Student and Campus Life Contribution (CVEC) payment or exemption proof  
  - Visa and permit documentation assistance through the university's International Welcome Desk  
- **Decision Timeline:** Admission decisions are typically communicated within two months after application closure.  
(Source: [Université Paris-Saclay Master’s Applications](http://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/en/admission/masters-applications-and-enrolment))

### 1.5 Enrollment and Registration

- Accepted candidates must confirm admission on the INCEPTION platform or linked systems.  
- Complete administrative enrollment including payment of CVEC (approximately €95 in 2025).  
- Register for classes within the university’s academic system.  
- Students continuing from M1 to M2 may need to submit Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) documents.  
(Source: [Université Paris-Saclay Enrollment Overview](http://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/en/admission/masters-applications-and-enrolment))

### 1.6 Tuition Fees

- **EU Students:** Approximately €254 annually (official tuition for public universities)  
- **Non-EU Students:** Higher fees, generally between €4,000 to €6,000 per year, depending on the exact program and administrative decisions  
- Students are encouraged to seek scholarships or financial aid to cover non-tuition expenses such as living costs.  
(Source: Various official descriptions and student portals)

## 2. France Excellence Charpak Master’s Scholarship: Eligibility and Application Details

### 2.1 Scholarship Overview

The France Excellence Charpak Master’s Scholarship is an initiative of Campus France India to support Indian students pursuing full-time master’s programs at French universities, including the Université Paris-Saclay Master’s in Economics.

- **Target Group:** Indian citizens or Overseas Citizens of India (OCI) under age 30  
- **Eligible Programs:** Full-time master’s degrees conducted entirely in France (English or French taught)  
- **Duration:** One or two years depending on the length of the master’s program  
(Source: [Campus France India Charpak Scholarship](https://inde.campusfrance.org/charpak-master-s-scholarship))

### 2.2 Eligibility Criteria

- Indian nationality or OCI cardholder, under 30 years old at time of application  
- Completed or currently enrolled in an Indian higher education institution (preference for degrees earned in India)  
- Must have applied or hold admission to a master’s program in France, including Université Paris-Saclay  
- No mandatory French language proficiency but proficiency is an advantage  
- Cannot simultaneously hold other major scholarships exceeding €600/month or conflicting internships/studies  
(Source: [Charpak Scholarship Eligibility](https://leapscholar.com/blog/france-excellence-charpak-master-scholarship/))

### 2.3 Application Requirements and Required Documents

- **Online Application Link:** Applications are submitted through the official Campus France India portal – [IFI Scholarship Portal](http://ifi.scholarship.ifindia.in/) or [Campus France Charpak](https://inde.campusfrance.org/charpak-master-s-scholarship).  
- **Documents Required:**  
  - Passport copy  
  - Admission letter or application acknowledgement from the French university  
  - Academic transcripts (Bachelor’s degree and any subsequent studies)  
  - Curriculum Vitae  
  - Letters of recommendation (usually 2)  
  - Statement of Purpose (SOP) describing academic goals and motivation  
  - Language certificates if available (English/French) – not mandatory  
  - Additional documents may be requested per portal guidelines  
- **Statement of Purpose:** Highly scrutinized for originality; plagiarism or AI-generated texts are grounds for disqualification  
(Source: [Campus France Charpak Scholarship Application](https://www.inde.campusfrance.org/charpak-master-s-scholarship))

### 2.4 Selection Process

- Eligibility verification and academic excellence assessment  
- Review of applications, including SOP quality and consistency  
- Online interview (mandatory, no rescheduling) conducted between April 13 and April 30, 2026  
- Final scholarship allocation based on merit and interview performance  
- Scholarship privileges include the status of French Government Scholars  
(Source: [Charpak Scholarship Selection Process](https://leapscholar.com/blog/france-excellence-charpak-master-scholarship/))

### 2.5 Scholarship Benefits

- Monthly stipend of €860 (~₹77,389) to cover living expenses  
- Visa fee exemption for applicants from India  
- Waiver of Campus France application fee  
- Compulsory student social security coverage and additional health insurance  
- Assistance with finding affordable housing and access to CROUS student restaurants and cultural activities  
- Does not cover travel expenses or personal costs  
- Benefits valid for full duration of the master’s program (one or two years)  
(Source: [Campus France India Charpak Scholarship Benefits](https://www.buddy4study.com/scholarship/france-excellence-charpak-master-s-program-2025))

### 2.6 Important Deadlines

- **Application Deadline:** March 30, 2026, 23:59 IST for 2026 intake  
- **Interview Period:** April 13 to April 30, 2026 (mandatory online interview)  
- Scholarship recipients are notified shortly after interviews conclude.  
(Source: [Charpak Scholarship Timeline](https://inde.campusfrance.org/charpak-master-s-scholarship))

## 3. Other Scholarships Relevant to Master’s in Economics Students at Université Paris-Saclay

### 3.1 Université Paris-Saclay International Master’s Scholarships

Université Paris-Saclay offers its own merit-based scholarships of €10,000 per year plus support for travel and visa fees, open to international students including those applying to the Master in Economics.

- **Eligibility:** Admission to a Master’s program at Université Paris-Saclay, under 30 years old, and not holding other substantial scholarships  
- **Deadline:** Around May 9, 2025, one year prior to the academic year start  
- **Application:** Requires nomination by program coordinators or application per university guidelines  
- **Benefits:** Helps cover tuition and living costs, promoting academic excellence and progression towards doctoral studies  
(Note: This is independent from the Charpak scholarship but can be combined if conditions allow.)  
(Source: [Université Paris-Saclay Scholarships 2025](https://www.opportunitiesforafricans.com/the-universite-paris-saclay-masters-scholarships-2025-2026-for-international-study-in-france/))

## Summary

- **Master’s in Economics Admission at Université Paris-Saclay** requires a relevant bachelor’s degree, strong economics and quantitative background, English proficiency, and submission of academic records, motivation letter, CV, and recommendations through the official INCEPTION platform, with deadlines typically by May 2025 for 2025-2026 intake.  
- **France Excellence Charpak Master’s Scholarship** is aimed at Indian students admitted to French master’s programs, including Université Paris-Saclay’s Economics, requiring online application by March 30, 2026, with supporting documents including passport, admission proof, academic records, SOP, and undergoing a mandatory interview in April 2026.  
- The Charpak scholarship provides a monthly living stipend, visa support, insurance, and assistance with accommodation, while the university’s own scholarships offer significant tuition and living expense support.  
- Applicants are encouraged to prepare thoroughly, ensuring linguistic readiness, academic preparedness, and a strong, original motivation statement to maximize admission and scholarship chances.

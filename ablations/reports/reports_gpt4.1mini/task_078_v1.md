# 9-Day Thailand Travel Itinerary for 7 Travelers from Hyderabad  
**Budget:** ₹70,000 per person (₹18,000 for flights, ₹52,000 for accommodation, activities, transport, food)  
**Group Size:** 7 travelers  
**Key Requirements:**  
- At least 2 full days in rural or countryside Thailand  
- Group-appropriate accommodations (dorm/Airbnb) for 7  
- Mix of cultural, adventure, nightlife activities  
- Feasible transport options for group of 7  
- Detailed daily cost breakdown  
- Reliable info based on official tourism and reputable travel providers  

---

## Overview and Destination Selection

Thailand offers a rich blend of vibrant city life, cultural heritage, adventurous nature-based activities, and tranquil countryside. For a 9-day trip focused on a group of 7 travelers on a budget, a balanced itinerary combining **Bangkok (capital city), Chiang Mai (Northern cultural hub with rural environs), and Khao Sok (Southern rural adventure area)** is ideal. This route ensures:

- Easy international arrival and departure via Bangkok  
- Affordable domestic flights (Bangkok to Chiang Mai, Chiang Mai to Surat Thani)  
- Convenient rural experiences near Chiang Mai and Khao Sok National Park  
- Diverse activities: temples, markets, elephant sanctuaries, waterfalls, rafting, nightlife  
- Group-friendly accommodations (Airbnbs, hostels/dorms) widely available  
- Efficient, economical transport solutions  

This itinerary aligns with budget constraints and logistical feasibility and satisfies the criteria of cultural, adventure, rural, and nightlife experiences.

## Detailed 9-Day Itinerary with Budgets and Suggestions

---

### Day 1 – Arrival in Bangkok  
**Activities:**  
- Arrive early and transfer to accommodation  
- Afternoon visit Grand Palace, Wat Pho (Temple of Reclining Buddha)  
- Evening stroll in Khao San Road for nightlife and street food  

**Accommodation:**  
- Group Airbnb near Khao San Road or dormitory-style hostel with private rooms for 7 (approx. 600 THB/person)  

**Transport:**  
- Airport to city: Group taxi or Grab (~250 THB per vehicle)  
- City travel: BTS/MRT and short Grab rides (~100 THB per person)  

**Costs per person:**  
- Accommodation: 600 THB (~₹1,350)  
- Food: 300 THB (~₹675)  
- Transport: 150 THB (~₹340)  
- Activities (Grand Palace + Wat Pho tickets): 500 THB (~₹1,130)  

**Daily total:** ~₹3,495  

---

### Day 2 – Bangkok Exploration & Nightlife  
**Activities:**  
- Visit Jim Thompson House Museum, shopping at MBK or Chatuchak (weekends)  
- Evening: Dinner cruise on Chao Phraya River or rooftop bar (moderate budget)  

**Accommodation & Transport:** Same as Day 1  

**Costs per person:**  
- Accommodation: 600 THB (~₹1,350)  
- Food: 350 THB (~₹790)  
- Transport: 150 THB (~₹340)  
- Activities (Jim Thompson + Dinner Cruise): 800 THB (~₹1,800)  

**Daily total:** ~₹4,280

## Accommodation Suggestions for Groups of 7

**Bangkok:**  
- Airbnb entire home near Khao San Road or Sukhumvit suitable for 7 with kitchen and common areas (~₹1,500/night total per person)  
- Hostels like NapPark Hostel or similar dormitories with private rooms for groups  

**Chiang Mai:**  
- Private large Airbnb villas in Old City or Nimmanhaemin (~500-600 THB pp/night)  
- Hostels with family/dorm rooms like Green Tiger Vegetarian House  

**Khao Sok:**  
- Riverside group bungalows or floating raft houses (shared Airbnb/guesthouse formats) around Surat Thani (~500 THB pp/night)  
- Local eco-resorts offering dormitory rooms or group cottages  

All offer Wi-Fi, kitchens (to self-cater if desired), and are well-reviewed for group comfort and budgets [6][7][8].

---

## Transportation Options for a Group of 7

- **Airport Transfers:** Group taxi vans or private Grab vehicles (~250-400 THB per trip)  
- **Between Cities:** Domestic budget flights (AirAsia, Nok Air) are affordable and time-saving; group bookings recommended  
- **Within Cities:** Songthaews (shared pickup taxis) ideal for groups, plus Grab for convenience  
- **Rural Trips:** Private minivan rentals for day tours in Chiang Mai and Khao Sok  
- **Local:** Tuk-tuks for short trips, scooters if experienced (safety and licenses required)  

Reasonably priced and logistically feasible for 7 people, transports keep transit times efficient and manage costs [13][14][15][16].

## Activities Mix

- **Cultural:** Grand Palace & temples in Bangkok, Chiang Mai temples, Jim Thompson House, tribal villages in Doi Inthanon  
- **Adventure:** Elephant sanctuary & bamboo rafting, jungle trekking and lake tours at Khao Sok, Doi Inthanon hiking and waterfalls  
- **Nightlife:** Khao San Road, Chiang Mai Night Bazaar, Bangkok rooftop bars, Asiatique night market  

Activity pricing varies broadly but careful selection fits budget limits while ensuring rich experiences [21][22].

This itinerary fits the ₹52,000 budget per person for accommodation, activities, food, and local transport post international flights.

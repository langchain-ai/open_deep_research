## 1. Novae’s Consumer Debt Relief Service Model

### 1.1 Overview and Mission

Novae, established in 2014 and based in Georgia, positions itself as a fintech-driven consumer finance platform focusing on financial inclusion and empowerment of underserved populations. Its Debt Help program, launched in 2023, targets Americans with high unsecured consumer debt (notably credit cards, medical bills, private student loans, personal loans, payday loans, and collection accounts), aiming to reduce payments by approximately 40–50% through negotiated settlements. Novae’s mission includes enhancing financial literacy and providing access to capital and credit improvement tools [1][4].

### 1.2 Operational Mechanisms

- **Licensed Provider Partnerships:**  
  Novae partners with licensed debt resolution companies and finance attorneys nationwide who conduct creditor negotiations on clients’ behalf. Novae itself is not a debt collector but a fintech platform facilitating these licensed operations and ensuring regulatory compliance.

- **Debt Negotiation Process:**  
  Clients first complete a free 30-minute consultation and undergo a soft credit pull, which does not impact their credit scores. Debt eligible for negotiated settlements typically must total at least $7,500 aggregated with minimum individual debts of $250. Once enrolled, negotiators liaise with creditors to reach settlements where creditors agree to accept less than owed to recover partial payment — a standard industry practice.

- **Fee Structure and Compliance:**  
  Fees range from 14% to 25% of the settled debt amount and are only charged post-successful debt resolution, adhering to the FTC’s Telemarketing Sales Rule (16 C.F.R. Part 310). Clients receive settlements usually within 4–7 months of entry, with ongoing settlements every 3–6 months, and typical program durations between 20 and 48 months.

- **Customer Transparency and Reporting:**  
  Novae discloses to clients the risks, including temporary credit score impacts, potential tax consequences of forgiven debt, and offers continuous account access to track progress. The company maintains Better Business Bureau accreditation, AFCC membership, and IAPDA compliance [1][4][5][11].

- **Technology Integration:**  
  Novae also offers AI-driven credit management products (e.g., myNovaeDisputes) aimed at empowering consumers to contest credit report errors independently, though these are distinct from their debt relief services.

### 1.3 Industry Recognition and Compliance

Novae is recognized repeatedly on the Inc. 5000 list for rapid growth and retains high ethical standards as a member of the Direct Selling Association, ensuring affiliates and partners operate under strict marketing and operational guidelines to prevent deceptive practices [3][11].

## 2. Novae’s Business Credit-Building Programs

### 2.1 Program Structure and Offerings

Novae’s approach to business credit focuses on enabling entrepreneurs and small businesses to build independent credit profiles separate from personal credit. Their Business Credit Builder program includes:

- **Product Tiers:**  
  - DIY package (~$995)  
  - Guided coaching package (~$2,495), including vendor connections, credit builder coaching, and access to proprietary resources.

- **Key Steps Emphasized:**  
  - Formation of legal business entities (LLCs, corporations)  
  - Acquisition of a business Employer Identification Number (EIN)  
  - Opening bank accounts and credit lines exclusively under the business name  
  - Establishing vendor trade credit relationships reporting to business credit bureaus  
  - Ongoing monitoring of business credit reports for accuracy and fundability

- **Funding Access:**  
  Successfully building business credit can unlock funding opportunities from $5,000 to $150,000+ without drawing on the owner’s SSN. Novae also facilitates access to business loan products, including SBA loans, CDFI loans, invoice factoring, equipment financing, and commercial real estate loans, primarily targeting underserved markets [6][7][8][10].

### 2.2 Separation of Business and Personal Credit

- **Legal and Financial Separation:**  
  Novae emphasizes strict separation to protect personal assets and build business creditworthiness, advising clients on best practices to avoid personal credit impacts aside from cases where personal guarantees are required.

- **Educational Focus:**  
  The program educates clients on the distinction between personal and business credit profiles, typical reporting agencies involved (e.g., Dun & Bradstreet, Experian Business, Equifax Business), and the mechanics of credit building distinct from personal borrowing behavior.

- **Benefits:**  
  This separation supports improved loan terms, higher line limits, and reduced personal financial risk, foundational for growing businesses seeking independent financing structures [18][19][20].

### 2.3 Operational and Compliance Framework

- Novae operates as a fintech company with a white-label, co-brand, and affiliate partner network distributing these programs to entrepreneurs and professionals.  
- Licensed lenders and financing partners provide underwriting and compliance oversight for business credit and loan products offered through Novae’s platform.  
- Training and marketing materials ensure affiliates comply with ethical standards and regulatory requirements, mitigating liability through robust contractual indemnifications [9][11][15].

## 3. Novae’s Partnership with Digital Estate Planning Platforms (Trust & Will)

### 3.1 Integration and Service Delivery

- Novae partners with **Trust & Will**, a leading digital estate planning platform, to offer state-specific, customizable Will-Based and Trust-Based estate planning services integrated within Novae’s fintech ecosystem.
- Clients access user-friendly online interfaces allowing completion of estate plans (including Last Will & Testament, Power of Attorney, Living Will, HIPAA authorization, and revocable living trusts) in approximately 15–20 minutes.
- Upon plan completion, clients can download digital copies immediately and receive complimentary first-time physical shipping of documents. Notarization for self-proving wills is recommended but optional, enhancing convenience and legal validity [1][6][8].

### 3.2 Pricing Models and Consumer Options

- Pricing for estate planning through Novae/Trust & Will starts around:
  - $199 for individual Will-Based plans.  
  - $299 for couples’ Will-Based plans.  
  - $499 for individual Trust-Based plans.  
  - $599 for couples’ Trust-Based plans.  
- Interest-free payment plans are available, allowing consumers to pay in four installments after completing documents.  
- Novae’s White Label Partners may set customized pricing above these base levels if providing these services under their own brand, reflecting flexible delivery agreements [1][5][8].

### 3.3 Strategic and Competitive Context

- Trust & Will emphasizes continuous estate plan reviews, advisor-client collaboration, deadline tracking, and asset ownership management tools integrated into broader wealth management workflows.
- This partnership enables Novae to offer comprehensive financial planning services beyond credit and lending, expanding touchpoints with underserved communities.
- The digital estate planning market is experiencing rapid innovation with AI and automation technologies enhancing client engagement and compliance but Novae’s current model centers on scalable, user-friendly online documents delivered via Trust & Will’s platform [2][4][6].

### 4.1 Explicit Evidence from Mesa Group Consulting’s Public Disclosures

- **Primary Source of Affiliate Mapping:**  
  The mapping of Mesa Group’s website pages to affiliate partners SuperMoney and Novae is derived explicitly from Mesa Group Consulting’s own website disclosures, including the homepage, privacy policy, terms & conditions, disclaimers, and specific service pages.

- **Partner Mentions:**

  - **SuperMoney** is characterized on the site as the affiliate for “credit cards, personal loans, auto loans, student loan refinancing, and debt consolidation” [1][2][4][11].

- **Service Page Alignment:**  
  Mesa Group’s website divides service pages logically; consumer loan and credit-related offerings (credit cards, personal loans, auto refinancing) link to SuperMoney, which specialises in consumer loan marketplaces.  
  In contrast, debt relief (personal and business), trust & will estate planning, life insurance, business debt relief, and business credit are attributed to Novae’s affiliate/fintech platform.

- While there was mention of a “Miro board” showing new pages with affiliate labels, no publicly available Miro boards or formal affiliate product catalogs were found externally that map Mesa Group’s pages to SuperMoney or Novae.  
- The publicly visible, legally mandated affiliate disclosures and policy statements on Mesa Group’s website serve as the **most reliable, direct evidence** substantiating the affiliate mapping.

### 4.3 Rationale for the Mapping Assignments

- **SuperMoney’s Role:**  
  As a financial product comparison and marketplace platform specialized in consumer loan products, SuperMoney naturally aligns with individual consumer loan product pages honest to their strategic model and promotional materials.

- **Novae’s Role:**  
  Novae’s broad fintech ecosystem, which includes debt relief, business credit, business debt relief, estate planning, and insurance, corresponds logically with Mesa Group’s affiliate pages covering these areas.

- **Business Model Consistency:**

### 4.4 Conclusion on Methodological Transparency

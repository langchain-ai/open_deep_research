## 2. SuperMoney: Company Profile and Product Offerings

### Company Background

SuperMoney is a California-based financial technology company founded in 2013 by Miron Lulic. Initially a personal finance blog, it evolved into a comprehensive financial marketplace and personal finance "super app." As of 2026, SuperMoney provides tools for users to compare and apply for various financial products with transparency and ease.

The company processes billions in loan requests annually and serves consumers and small businesses with a focus on no-fee financing, clarity, and reducing financial stress. SuperMoney has been recognized on the Inc. 5000 list and Deloitte Technology Fast 500, and won awards for financial product comparison services in 2023 and 2024 [4][7][10][19].

### Financial Products

SuperMoney offers a wide range of financial products through its platform, enabling users to compare offers from multiple lenders instantly via single applications:

- **Credit Cards**: Offers a marketplace with comparison tools for credit cards from various issuers.
- **Personal Loans**: Options including debt consolidation loans with rates starting around 6.53% APR.
- **Auto Loan Refinancing**: Tools to refinance car loans with competitive rates.
- **Student Loan Refinancing**: Launched in 2018, featuring fixed rates from 1.00%–14.00%, variable from 4.42%–14.00%.
- **Debt Consolidation Loans**: Designed to bundle and reduce debt burden.
- **Debt Relief**: Partnerships offer debt management and negotiation services.
- **Trust & Will Plans and Life Insurance**: Comparisons and offerings supported.
- **Business Credit and Debt Relief**: Financing solutions for small-to-medium enterprises (SMEs), including no-fee financing programs.

SuperMoney emphasizes soft credit checks for pre-qualification, fast funding (sometimes within one business day), and supports user education with reviews and financial guidance [4][6][10][12][15].

### Affiliate Program

SuperMoney runs robust affiliate programs enabling partners to monetize website traffic by promoting SuperMoney’s financial products. Key features:

- Multiple verticals: credit reporting, personal loans, student refinancing, business financing, credit repair.
- Commission structures include CPA (Cost Per Action) and RevShare models.
- Payouts vary from $15 per credit report to $750 per funded loan; business financing commissions may reach 80% per sale on revenue share.
- Affiliates gain access to embeddable widgets, co-branded offer engines, and prequalification tools to enhance conversions.
- The programs are free to join and partner with hundreds of financial brands [16][17][18][19][20].

### Fit with Mesa Group Consulting’s Focus

SuperMoney’s offerings align well with Mesa Group Consulting’s core areas: credit repair (via listings and credit monitoring tools), credit monitoring, and business credit/funding products. Mesa Group Consulting builds dedicated website pages to promote SuperMoney’s:

- Credit cards  
- Personal loans  
- Auto loan refinancing  
- Student loan refinancing  
- Debt consolidation loans  

Thus, Mesa Group acts as an affiliate or content partner leveraging SuperMoney’s extensive product portfolio and affiliate tools for lead generation and client solutions, driving revenue through commissions while helping clients access diverse financial products efficiently [3][5][12].

## 3. Novae: Company Profile, Business Model, and Financial Services

### Company Overview and Business Model

Novae LLC is a U.S.-based fintech company founded in 2014 by Reco McCambry. Its mission centers on empowering underserved communities by improving access to credit, capital, and entrepreneurship opportunities nationwide, including individuals and small businesses.

It operates as a fintech platform, consultant, and direct sales company, leveraging behavioral economics, AI credit management tools, and a "cellular" adaptable business model focused on user-centric financial services. Novae is recognized on the Inc. 5000 list for fastest-growing private companies five consecutive years (2021–2025).

Novae is distinct from similarly named entities in insurance (London-based Novae Group plc) and industrial engineering, with its core focus on fintech innovation via network marketing and affiliate partnerships [1][2][3][4][14][15].

### Financial Product and Service Offerings

Novae offers an extensive portfolio tailored to both individuals and small businesses:

**For Consumers:**

- Credit building (personal credit score improvement)  
- Debt relief programs (reduce monthly payments by up to 50%, debt-free in 20–48 months)  
- Personal loans, auto loan refinancing, student loan refinancing  
- Credit card offers and comparison  
- Life insurance shopping  
- Trust and will estate planning  
- Tax preparation with instant refund advances, up to $7,000  
- Savings accounts  
- No-interest payday advances (small cash loans)  
- Credit monitoring and dispute management through AI tools like myNovaeDisputes [6][7][8]

**For Small Businesses:**

- Business credit building and business loans (funding up to $10 million in some programs)  
- Business debt relief services, including partnerships with licensed debt restructuring firms  
- Government grants and specialized small business loan programs  
- Business registration, consumer financing, and free essential business services  
- Vacation incentives and entrepreneurial support programs, including church and community outreach models [6][10][11]

### Affiliate and White Label Programs

Novae strongly emphasizes affiliate marketing as a growth and revenue channel. Its Affiliate Program allows individuals and business owners to earn commissions and residual income by referring customers and other affiliates. Key points include:

- High commission potential (up to $3,000 per referral plus ongoing residuals)  
- Multi-level generational overrides (up to 10 levels)  
- Rewards such as luxury car bonuses and mortgage payments  
- Affiliate Basic ($199 upfront + $49/mo) and Affiliate Pro ($349 upfront + $99/mo) plans, the latter including credit monitoring tools and team-building commissions  
- Social mission component wherein a percentage of revenue supports underserved communities via "Novae Cares" [9][10]

Additionally, Novae offers a White Label Partner Program where businesses and entrepreneurs license Novae’s fintech software, brand, and methodology to operate independently under their own branding, offering Novae’s full product suite. This includes website development, billing, merchant account setup, marketing, training, and customer support. White Label partnerships allow revenue via service markups and commissions. This model broadens Novae’s reach through cooperative business ownership and technology licensing [16].

### Fit with Mesa Group Consulting’s Focus

Novae’s product offerings cover all categories Mesa Group Consulting references on new website pages assigned to "novae" affiliates:

- Debt relief (personal and business)  
- Trust and will plans  
- Life insurance  
- Business debt relief  
- Business credit  

Mesa Group Consulting likely leverages Novae’s affiliate or white label programs to promote these diverse financial products to their client base, especially targeting underserved or credit-challenged individuals and small businesses. This aligns well with Calderon’s credit repair and business funding expertise. The partnership provides Mesa Group access to scalable loan and credit product offerings, AI-driven credit management, and community-focused programs [3][4][6][8][10][16].

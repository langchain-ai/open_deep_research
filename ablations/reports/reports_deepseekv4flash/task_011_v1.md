[Behind MLM - Novae Money Review](https://behindmlm.com/mlm-reviews/novae-money-review-financial-services-rebrand-stock-fraud)
[Novae Money Compensation Plan PDF (August 2024)](https://novaemoneytraining.com/docs/Novae-Money-Comp-Plan.pdf)

**7 Ways to Get Paid:**

1. **Personal / Retail Commissions**: Earned when an affiliate sells Novae products or services directly. Commissions vary by product/service price and affiliate rank. Examples include up to $100 per referral for credit education and up to $500 per customer enrolled depending on the financial product.

2. **Customer Acquisition Bonuses (CABs)**: Paid to the sponsor and eligible up-line Affiliate Pros when a new Affiliate generates 2 Qualifying Customer Activities in their first 60 days. Commissions up to $400 per referral of new affiliates.

3. **Team Override Commissions**: Available exclusively to Affiliate Pro members, providing multi-generational income through up to ten generations. Payouts vary by product and generation (e.g., $3 for generations 1-5 and $2 for generations 6-10).

4. **Monthly Residual Income**: Paid on the 15th day of each month to affiliates who achieve certain ranks based on the previous month's sales volume.

5. **Rank Advancement Commissions**: Ranks range from Level 1 to Level 16 (or Affiliate through President Ambassador & Managing Partner). Higher ranks unlock higher commissions and bonuses.

6. **Bonuses**: Car bonuses and mortgage bonuses for top performers, plus rank achievement bonuses.

7. **Company Stock**: Novae promises "company stock" to top promoters, though it is not publicly traded, registered, or discloses valuation [Novae Compensation Plan - 7 Ways to Get Paid](https://novaemoney.com/base/compensation-plan)

**Commission Payment Schedule:**
- **Weekly commissions**: Paid on Fridays by 5pm PST for sales completed the prior week, with a minimum commission payment of $25
- **Monthly residual commissions**: Paid on the 15th of the month following qualifying sales volume
- **Payout methods**: Direct deposit or NovaePay accounts

**Income Disclosure:**
Novae's official income disclosure statement reports: "The average participant in this business earned $1,408 in 2023. Some earn less while some earn more" [Behind MLM - Novae Money Review](https://behindmlm.com/mlm-reviews/novae-money-review-financial-services-rebrand-stock-fraud).

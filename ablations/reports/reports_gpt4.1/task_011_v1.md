## Overview of SuperMoney

### Company Profile and Mission

SuperMoney is a US-based financial technology (fintech) platform founded in 2013, specializing in personal finance education, product comparisons, and tools to relieve financial stress. Its mission is to empower users to make better financial decisions by providing transparent access to a comprehensive marketplace of financial products and services [1][2][3].

### Services and Products

SuperMoney's offerings include:

- **Financial Product Comparison Marketplace**: Users can compare rates, features, and reviews for credit cards, personal loans, auto loans, student loan refinancing, mortgages, debt consolidation, debt relief, banking products, insurance, trust and will planning, and investment options [1][2][3].
- **Prequalification Tools**: The site allows users to submit a single application to receive pre-qualified offers across multiple lenders or providers.
- **AI-driven Personal Finance App**: As of 2025, includes automated budgeting, credit monitoring, account aggregation, and AI-powered financial guidance [1].
- **Consumer Reviews and Ratings**: Aggregates thousands of real customer experiences on financial products.
- **Financial Education**: Provides guides, articles, and calculators.

SuperMoney itself does not originate loans or hold user funds; it operates purely as an information and referral platform [3]. It is recognized in the fintech industry as a top product comparison tool [1][4].

## Overview of Novae

### Company Profile and Mission

Novae, operating nationally via [novaemoney.com](https://novaemoney.com), is a fintech consulting company orienting toward financial inclusion, especially within underserved communities [5]. Founded in 2014, Novae provides an array of consumer and business-focused financial services and positions itself as a leader in financial empowerment and education.

### Services and Products

Novae's major offerings include:

- **Consumer Services**: 
  - Credit repair and disputes (MyNovaeDisputes)
  - Debt reduction programs and debt consolidation
  - Credit card and personal loan offers
  - No-interest payday loans, tax refund advances
  - Life insurance shopping, high-yield savings, online will and trust creation
  - Auto and student loan refinancing, credit monitoring platforms [5]
- **Business Services**:
  - Business credit, funding, and grants
  - Business consumer financing, business registration, and debt relief [5]
- **Education and Affiliate Programs**: 
  - Financial literacy materials, business training, and events for both consumers and affiliates [6][7]
  
Novae is a member of several business and industry organizations and operates with documented affiliate and independent contractor agreements [6][7].

## Affiliate Programs: SuperMoney and Novae

### SuperMoney Affiliate Program

SuperMoney operates a robust affiliate program that enables digital marketers, financial consultants, and comparison sites to refer customers and earn commissions [8][9].

- **Product Scope**: Affiliates can earn by promoting credit cards, personal loans, auto loans, student loan refinancing, debt consolidation, debt relief, mortgages, insurance, banking products, trust and will planning, and more [8][9][10].
- **Integration**: SuperMoney provides embeddable comparison widgets, prequalification forms, and API integrations so affiliates can natively present SuperMoney offers on their own websites [8].
- **Compensation Model**:
  - Commissions are paid per qualified lead, application submission, and/or successfully funded product, depending on the vertical.
  - Examples: Up to $750 per funded sale for certain products; or cost-per-action (CPA) per eligible application [8][10].
  - Earnings per click (EPC) are highlighted as a program advantage [9][10].

SuperMoney’s program is open to qualified partners and is managed via internal systems and partner networks such as FlexOffers [10].

### Novae Affiliate Program

Novae provides a structured, multi-level affiliate program with multiple options for engagement and compensation [6][11][12][13].

- **Product Scope**: Affiliates may promote credit repair, personal loans, debt reduction, credit cards, trust and will planning, life insurance, business credit, business funding, business debt relief, tax services, and more [5][6][12][13].
- **Program Structure**:
  - Several affiliate tiers (D.I.Y, Affiliate, Affiliate Plus) with increasing price and included services
  - Training, tools, and credit monitoring are included at higher levels [12].
- **Compensation Model**:
  - Retail commissions on direct product/service sales
  - Customer Acquisition Bonuses (CABs)
  - Residual monthly income based on group sales/team activity
  - Additional team-building incentives and event commissions [12][13].
  - Detailed payout structures are documented in Novae’s official compensation plan PDF [12].
  - Payouts are subject to commission thresholds and are paid weekly, with monthly residuals.

Affiliates operate as independent contractors and are subject to compliance requirements; no guaranteed results are promised [6][12].

## Compensation and Benefits for Affiliates

### SuperMoney

- **Compensation is performance-driven:** Affiliates earn on a cost-per-action (CPA) model—commissions are paid for each qualified lead (e.g., completed loan or insurance application) referred via affiliate links, widgets, or embedded tools [8][9][10].
- **High potential payouts:** Some verticals (like funded loans or debt relief) can earn up to $750 per successful transaction [10].
- **Affiliate dashboard and reporting:** Affiliates, like Mesa Group Consulting, can track their leads and commissions via SuperMoney’s affiliate platform.

### Novae

- **Multi-level compensation:** Affiliates make retail commissions from direct product referrals, bonuses for acquiring new affiliates, and earn ongoing residuals based on the performance and size of their downline/team [11][12][13].
- **Structured, incentive-driven payouts:** Commissions are paid both weekly (for immediate actions) and monthly (for residual income).
- **Service-specific bonuses:** Additional compensation for certain high-value services (especially business funding and debt relief).
- **No guarantee of credit improvement:** Novae states explicitly that affiliate earnings and client results are not guaranteed [11][12].

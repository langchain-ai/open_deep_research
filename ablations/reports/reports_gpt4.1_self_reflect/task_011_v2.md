# Comprehensive Analysis of the Business and Affiliate Relationships Among Mesa Group Consulting, SuperMoney, and Novae

## Introduction

This report presents a detailed, evidence-based analysis of the business and affiliate relationships among Mesa Group Consulting (operated by Evert Calderon), SuperMoney, and Novae. The analysis covers the products and services offered by SuperMoney and Novae, specifics of their affiliate programs—including compensation, tools, participation requirements—how Mesa Group Consulting leverages these partnerships, the legal/compliance frameworks governing their affiliate relationships, and identifies any information gaps or limitations in the publicly available record.

---

## 1. Products and Services: SuperMoney and Novae

### 1.1 SuperMoney

SuperMoney is a leading U.S.-based fintech company founded in 2013 and headquartered in Santa Ana, California. It operates as an independent financial services comparison platform and fintech marketplace, facilitating consumers’ access to financial products without directly offering loans or holding deposits.

#### Key Products & Services Relevant to the Research Brief

- **Financial Product Comparison Marketplaces:** 
  - **Personal Loans:** Unsecured loans from $500–$100,000, APRs starting at 5.99%, with prequalification available for all credit ranges.
  - **Debt Relief and Debt Consolidation:** Platforms for debt relief (minimum credit score 300, APRs up to 39.95%) and debt consolidation offers.
  - **Auto Loans & Refinancing:** APRs from 2.99%, terms up to 84 months.
  - **Student Loan Refinancing:** Multiple lending partners; rates and eligibility vary.
  - **Credit Cards and Banking Products:** Comprehensive overview and comparison of credit card and bank account offers.
  - **Business Loans and Funding:** Access to business loans, lines of credit, invoice factoring, merchant cash advances, commercial mortgages, and business credit cards.
  - **Insurance:** Comparisons for life, auto, and other personal/business insurance products.
  - **Tax Relief and Estate Planning:** Tools and partner referrals for tax debt and trust/estate creation.
- **AI-Powered Personal Finance App:** Automated budgeting, account aggregation, goal setting, net worth tracking, and real-time credit monitoring integrated as a paid subscription ($9.99/month after a free trial).
- **Prequalification & Application Tools:** Single form applications deliver multiple prequalified offers from partner institutions.
- **Consumer Reviews & Education:** Thousands of verified product reviews, guides, and tips to educate users.
- **Debt Relief and Credit Repair Info:** Marketplace includes reputable debt settlement companies and credit help resources.
  
SuperMoney’s coverage is national, with offerings updated regularly and no user-facing costs for most comparison tools. All revenue is generated through commissions from partners upon successful referrals, lead generation, or completed transactions[1][2][3][4][5][6].

### 1.2 Novae

Novae, LLC is a fintech and business services company launched in 2014 and headquartered in Georgia. Novae’s mission is to promote financial inclusion and entrepreneurship, particularly among underserved U.S. communities. The company operates nationwide and is recognized on the Inc. 5000 list for its growth and innovation.

#### Key Products & Services Relevant to the Research Brief

- **Consumer Financial Services:**
  - **Debt Help and Relief:** Debt settlement, negotiation with creditors, consolidation, and structured repayment programs (both personal and small business).
  - **Credit Services:** (a) Credit monitoring (myNovaeCredit, with multi-bureau reports, credit improving simulators, identity theft insurance, and privacy management); (b) DIY dispute management software (myNovaeDisputes); (c) Loan, credit card, and savings product matching.
  - **Loans and Credit Cards:** Personal loan matching up to $100,000, auto/student loan refinancing, credit card offers.
  - **Insurance:** Life, health, and related consumer insurance products.
  - **Tax Preparation and Planning:** IRS-certified professionals, refund advances, and personal/business tax services.
  - **Trust & Estate Planning:** Online trusts and wills, state-specific documentation, and education.
- **Business Solutions:**
  - **Business Funding & Grants:** Term loans, cash advances, commercial real estate, equipment financing, merchant cash advance, invoice factoring, with offers up to $150 million.
  - **Business Debt Relief:** Programs targeting business debts over $15,000 (including asset protection and restructuring).
  - **Business Credit Building:** Stepwise training on obtaining and growing business credit.
  - **Business Registration and Formation:** LLC setup and compliance tools.
- **Tech/Fulfillment & Entrepreneurial Programs:**
  - White label and co-branded product licensing, integrated marketing support, education, and fintech training.
- **Educational Resources:** Via “Novae University” for both consumers and affiliates on credit, business, and financial growth.

Novae positions itself as both a direct-to-consumer and business service provider with a substantial affiliate/partner network[7][8][9][10][11][12][13].

## 2. Affiliate Programs: Mechanics and Participation

### 2.1 SuperMoney Affiliate Program

SuperMoney offers a comprehensive affiliate program targeting publishers, comparison websites, content creators, and financial consultants.

#### Compensation Models

- **Per Lead / Action Commissions:** 
  - Payouts per qualified lead, application, or completed sale.
  - For personal loans, debt relief, and business financing: up to $750–$1500 per funded loan/sale.
  - In tax verticals: up to $50 per qualified lead.
- **Earnings Per Click (EPC):** Noted to have high EPC due to broad lender/partner integrations and optimized conversion.
- **No Cost to Affiliate for Program Participation:** Registration open via internal platform and networks such as FlexOffers[14][15][16][17][18].

#### Affiliate Tools

- **Super Links:** Trackable affiliate URLs/co-branded landing pages.
- **Money Marketplace:** Embeddable product/comparison widgets for websites.
- **Prequalification Platform:** White-labeled or embedded prequalification/application forms for traffic capture or on-site experiences.
- **APIs and Integration Support:** For higher-tier partners with custom needs.
- **Reporting Dashboard:** Affiliates can monitor clicks, leads, commissions in real time.
- **Content Hub:** Library of compliant articles and guides for further monetization.

#### Requirements for Participation

- Open to publishers and marketers in the U.S.; must agree to Terms of Service.
- Marketing must comply with SuperMoney’s content standards and legal requirements.
- No direct, detailed public-facing affiliate enrollment agreement or explicit compliance training is available—affiliates generally adhere to mainstream performance marketing compliance and the broader platform’s terms of service[18][19][20].

### 2.2 Novae Affiliate Program

Novae operates a structured, multi-level affiliate marketing model supporting both individual affiliates and white-label partners.

#### Compensation Models

- **Direct Retail Commissions:** On personal sales of Novae products/services (credit monitoring, business funding, debt help, etc.).
- **Team Override Commissions:** For Affiliate Pro members, overrides on team/deep network sales (up to 10 levels/generations).
- **Customer Acquisition Bonuses (CABs):** Recruitment incentive for enrolling new affiliate members.
- **Residual Monthly Income:** Based on qualified volume and rank (with detailed rank and income structure available, from $50/mo for entry to $110,000/mo for top-tier network leaders).
- **White Label Licensing:** Enhanced revenue/mark-up options for partners licensing Novae’s tech stack under their own branding.

#### Affiliate Tools

- **Dedicated Landing Pages and Templates:** Customizable, company-approved sales websites/templates for product promotion.
- **White-label Platform:** For qualifying partners, full technology stack, custom branding, dedicated merchant account, billing, and product packaging.
- **Sales and Marketing Aids:** Corporate-supplied materials; no unauthorized or self-built promotional/ad content allowed without written approval.
- **Affiliate Dashboard:** For lead tracking, commissions, customer/referral management.

#### Requirements for Participation

- Age 18+, with valid US tax identification, and reside in the US or an approved location.
- Enrollment slides: Affiliate BASIC ($199 sign-up, $49/mo); Affiliate PRO ($349 sign-up, $99/mo) for enhanced features and earning potential.
- Affiliates must sign the comprehensive Affiliate Agreement and adhere to the Policies and Procedures, as well as the Compensation Plan.
- Mandatory use of company-issued marketing collateral; independent sites require written company review/approval.
- Adherence to strict compliance on income claims, non-solicitation, advertising standards, and anti-fraud provisions[21][22][23][24][25][26][27][28][29].

## 3. Mesa Group Consulting’s Use of SuperMoney and Novae Partnerships

### Explicit Documentation of Partnerships

- Mesa Group Consulting (run by Evert Calderon) discloses on multiple public pages its affiliate and referral relationships with both SuperMoney and Novae[30][31][32][33][34][35].
- Disclosures appear on the homepage, About, legal, Terms, and privacy policy pages: 
    - Citing SuperMoney as a partner for “financial product comparisons, personal loans, auto loans.”
    - Naming Novae as partner for “business funding and debt relief (business and personal).”
    - Repeated policies state: “We may receive compensation through affiliate partnerships and referral arrangements including SmartCredit, SuperMoney, and Novae.”[30][31][32][36]
- The consumer and business service pages reiterate:
    - SuperMoney linkages for: credit cards, personal and auto loans, debt consolidation, and student loan refinancing.
    - Novae linkages for: debt relief, trust and will planning, life insurance, business credit, and business debt relief[30][31][36].

### Operational Mechanics (As Publicly Visible)

- The website uses standard affiliate/compliance legal phrasing but does **not** show direct integration of affiliate tools (widgets, embedded forms) in public-facing product pages. There are no visible SuperMoney widgets, API calls, nor unique co-branded Novae pages embedded[30][31].
- Privacy and cookie policies specifically note tracking for referrals and affiliate arrangements, e.g., “affiliate tracking cookies (track referrals from affiliate partners).”[33]
- No visible third-party dashboards, direct tracking URLs, or Miro board links are present on the live site.
- There is no evidence of public-facing, technical integration of unique partner tools, nor displays of referral dashboards, payout structures, or actual tracked links on the main site.
- The mode of client referral—whether by private consultations, direct links, or emailing clients referral offers—is not detailed on public pages.
- All available legal sections (privacy, disclaimers, terms, and business/consumer pages) contain the appropriate affiliate compensation disclosures required by law, but lack specifics on operational flow[30][31][34][36].

## 4. Legal and Compliance Requirements for Affiliates

### 4.1 SuperMoney

- **Platform Terms of Service:** 
    - The overarching [Terms of Service](https://www.supermoney.com/tos) governs all use and participation, including by affiliate partners.
    - Sets legal standards for identity verification, consumer protection, dispute resolution (binding individual arbitration), limitations on liability, disclaimers, third-party relationships, and intellectual property[19][20].
    - Users and partners must comply with all governing laws; SuperMoney emphasizes that it does not provide financial advice, only impartial comparison tools.

- **Affiliate Program-Specific Rules:** 
    - No separately published, detailed affiliate agreement or compliance manual is available in the public record.
    - FlexOffers and private onboarding may have additional, private agreements, but these are not openly accessible.

- **Privacy Policy:** 
    - Details personal information sharing, data retention, affiliate data use, user rights under state and federal privacy statutes, and cross-partner referral information[37].
    - Platform prohibits unauthorized or misleading use of marketing, underlines truthful representation, and obligates all partners to comply with applicable privacy/advertising laws.

### 4.2 Novae

- **Affiliate Agreement and Enrollment:** 
    - [Affiliate Enrollment Agreement](https://novaemoneytraining.com/wp-content/uploads/Affiliate_Enrollment_Agreement.pdf) requires personal information, agreement to the program's terms, and completion of at least one personal sale[21][24][25].
    - Affiliates are independent contractors, not employees, and are responsible for all applicable taxes.

- **Policies and Procedures:** 
    - [Policies and Procedures document](https://novaemoneytraining.com/wp-content/uploads/2020_Novae_PandPs.pdf) covers:
        - Approved marketing materials and restrictions on custom/unapproved content.
        - Prohibitions on unlawful/unethical conduct, misleading claims, cross-solicitation, and bonus buying.
        - Advertising rules, trademark usage, social media conduct, online compliance, and disclosure requirements for income/opportunity representations.
        - Annual renewal/termination clauses, dispute resolution (arbitration in Georgia), confidentiality requirements, and intellectual property handling.
    - Explicit provision that no affiliate is required to purchase products/services to join, provided a written request is made.

- **Compensation Plan:** 
    - [Compensation Plan PDF](https://novaemoneytraining.com/docs/Novae-Money-Comp-Plan.pdf) dictates all commission, bonus, and rank advancement structure[27].

- **Privacy Policy:** 
    - Outlines scope and sharing of personal and financial data, opt-out rights, and use of cookies for tracking[38].

- **No live examples of click-tracking or unique referral paths.**
- **No available Miro boards, detailed internal agreements, or referral dashboards visible to the public.**
- **No specifics on commission structure or actual payout history for Mesa Group Consulting:** The nature and size of compensation (volume, amounts, or performance benchmarks) remain private.
- **No affiliate program manuals for SuperMoney are publicly available:** Novae has published compliance documents, but SuperMoney’s program details (beyond general payout levels and public landing pages) are not explicitly disseminated.
- **No operational mechanics for referrals:** The practical method (link, in-consultation, email referral, etc.) for connecting Mesa Group clients to affiliate products is not documented on public web pages.
- **No evidence of formal employment, back-office integration, or exclusive contracts:** All public information supports an affiliate/referral partnership, not deeper operational or brand integration.

## Conclusion

SuperMoney and Novae are both established, highly regulated financial technology and service platforms with extensive product coverage across credit repair, credit monitoring, loans, insurance, debt relief, business funding, trust/estate planning, and related financial offerings. Both operate robust affiliate programs:

- **SuperMoney** leverages performance-based affiliate placements (per-lead, per-action, per-funded-loan), with numerous tools for publishers (widgets, APIs, co-branded pages), and compliance frameworks built into their general platform terms and privacy policies. Up to $750–$1500 per completed loan/funded sale is documented for high-value verticals, with simple signup for U.S. partners[15][16][17][18].
- **Novae** operates a tiered, multi-level commission-driven program for both individuals and larger white-label partners, with detailed compliance, approved marketing assets, team overrides, residual incomes, and full transparency around contractual/legal terms. Team-building bonuses and rank advancement offer significant income opportunities, governed by detailed agreements, policies, and compliance documentation[21][22][27][28][29].

Mesa Group Consulting (run by Evert Calderon) is transparently leveraging these platforms for the monetization of its financial consulting business—referring clients to SuperMoney for lending, consolidation, and credit products, and to Novae for debt relief, trust/estate, business credit, and related services. This is established through multiple legal and policy disclosures on Mesa Group's public site, though without detailed public documentation of their technical integration, commission structure, or referral implementation.

The compliance frameworks for both affiliate programs are robust, requiring full legal adherence, detailed consumer disclosures, and strong limitations on marketing claims and conduct. Novae’s compliance policies are published and granular; SuperMoney’s guidelines are contained in broader user/partner terms with substantial, but less affiliate-specific, public documentation.

Key gaps remain: the public record provides no live view of affiliate referral mechanics, tracking, or the precise compensation Mesa Group Consulting earns from these relationships.
